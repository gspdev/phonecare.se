Free Nationwide Shipping With Courier

We make it simple and affordable to get your device repaired no matter where you live in Sweden.

When we receive your device we will diagnose the exact problem or problems and inform you of the cost to repair it. If you agree with the suggested repair, your device will be repaired and sent back to you. If you decide not to go ahead with the suggested repair or your device is deemed "not repairable", there will be a SEK 100 diagnostic fee charged to you.

Just follow the simple instructions below and in most cases your device will be repaired and back in your hand in about 48 hours.

 *Note* Shipping and delivery is free for every repair. In case your device is not fixable or is fixable but you decide not to repair, you are charged only the diagnostic fee (SEK 100). For laptops with a repair cost less than SEK 700, you are charged the cost of the repair plus the shipping cost (SEK 100).


4 Simple Steps for nationwide repairs in 48 hours

  • Print and fill out our repair form

    Download the form here. If you can't print the form, just send a paper with your device with your name, address, phone number and briefly describe the problem with your device. By sending your device you agree to the terms and conditions in the form.

     

  • Send your device

    Please call G-SP at 559001-3727 to find the nearest location. When you drop off your device, ask them to charge Phonecare for the shipping fees. Shipping is free for all packages under 3kg and highly discounted for larger packages.

    Note: Please remove and keep your SIM card before sending your device. In case your battery is removable (ex.Samsung) please remove it and send it separately along with your device.
    Phonecare
    Kungsgatan 29, 111 56 Stockholm

     

  • Inform and repair

    We will contact you to inform you the cost of your repair. After approval, we will immediately repair and ship back your device the same day for most repairs. Motherboard repairs, liquid damage and a few other difficult repairs can take from 1-7 days to ship back.

     

  • Make payment

    You can pay the courier when your device is delivered. If you choose to pay through bank transfer or PayPal. Your device will be shipped after the payment has been received.
